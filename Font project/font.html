10 commandmands of typography

  1. DO NOT apply more than three typefaces
  2. DO NOT enlarge headlines at the top of the page
  3. 8px to 10pt for body
  4. if typeface is not legible it is truly not a typeface
  5. Honor kerning ,white space must be equalized
  6. DO NOT stress discreetly upon elements with text
  7. DO NOT use capitals when setting body
  8. always align letters and words on baseline
  9. Use flush-left,ragged-right alignment
  10. DO NOT make lines too short or too long
